About DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO

Program Overview

DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO is a Performance Contract system designed at the family level. It enables families to establish shared commitments and track collective achievements while recognizing individual family member contributions.

Kibungo Sector Initiative

This application supports Kibungo Sector's population development goals by providing a structured framework for family-level performance management and accountability.

System Features

Family Information

Record complete information for each family member and manage all household data in one centralized system.

Individual Performance Duties

Each family member has specific sub-performance duties that contribute to the family's overall Performance Contract (Imihigo).

Shared Family Goals

Set family-level performance duties with shared responsibility among all family members for collective achievement.

How to Use the System

Access the system through the link below. You can enter data yourself or seek assistance from an Intore mu Ikoranabuhanga (technology facilitator) or your administrative authority.

Register your family and enter detailed information for each family member including names, roles, and responsibilities.

Define individual performance duties for each member and establish shared family performance goals (Imihigo).

Monitor individual and family performance, track achievements, and update progress regularly.
